About

At Style Cave, we believe that, at its best, a barbershop is dependable, trusted neighbourhood institution.

Our primary goal is to build a strong and long-term relationship with each of our clients.

Style Cave barbershop is proud to be an independent, Belgrade-bred and operated store focused solely on serving the Belgrade market. Our unparalleled team of expert barbers has personal pride riding on each haircut and hot lather shave. No one on Style Cave staff has been cutting hair fewer than 10 years. We've seen it all and can do it all. We are about substance and skill, not gimmicks. We like to have fun, but we take our work seriously. Sean's story

Sean - hails from Middlesex County and has had a passion for cutting hair since he was a kid. He honed his craft back in high school where he kept an ever-increasing number of classmates looking sharp. His signature cut is a classic combover with detailed scissor work. Shane
Master Barber

Shane's story

Shane - reared in Middlesex County and worked as a Master Barber instructor at the New England Hair Academy before joining the team at Tweed. His specialties are fades and precise scissor cuts. He's a devoted follower of all Boston sports teams, so for best results don't wear a Yankees' cap when you come to see him.
